What are Phoenix Leagues?

Phoenix Comics NW likes to add a little bit more excitement and competition to their weekly events.

Every night you show up to play you earn points that count towards your final score (at no additional cost / you do NOTHING)! If you're interested in learning more please let us know in store or over Facebook!
