What are Phoenix Leagues?

Phoenix Comics NW likes to add a little bit more excitement and competition to their weekly events. Every night you show up to play you earn points that count towards your final score! If you're interested in learning more please let us know in store or over Facebook!


Current Leagues started on the 3rd (Legacy), 7th (Modern) and 9th (CEDH) of January and will run for 8 weeks.
