What are Phoenix Leagues?

Phoenix Comics NW likes to add a little bit more excitement and competition to their weekly events.

Every night you show up to play during a League, you will earn points that count towards your final score. Being a part of the League costs nothing extra and is done automatically!
Keep reading if you're interested in learning more. Or chat with us in store or over Facebook!
